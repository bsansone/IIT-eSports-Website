Events

We are excited to see the potential that Illinois Tech has in the eSports world. Scarlet League allows participants to play with their peers and introduce the fascinating realities and complexities of eSports gaming through these matches. Our wish is that when the day(s) are done, you will have played against students of varying levels and developed a strong camaraderie alongside your team.

All Illinois Tech Students, IIT alumni, and registered students from other Universities, are invited to this 5v5 tournament to compete on the Fields of Justice. Check out our 'Tournament' Tab to know more about the tournament format and rules, schedule of events, and prizes available (which will be constantly updated).

Created and hosted by IIT Biitcoin Chicago, and Smash Bros club (as well as a generous donation from Union Board), this tournament is the best way to show who is the best in the Chicago. Through a day full of matches, hilarious commentary, and ganks galore, we look forward to working with you and your team to ensure you get the most out of this experience.

So grab your friends, mice, keyboards and gaming mechanics and see you all on the rift!

Update: If you'd like to play practice matches with the IIT community and/or find other players at IIT, please join the Illinois Tech club in game and the ScarletLeague Facebook page.
